Simple 5-Minute Meditation

1Find a quiet spot. Sit comfortably. Chair or floor.
2Set a timer. Start with just 5 minutes.
3Close your eyes. Relax your shoulders.
4Focus on your breath. Notice the inhale and exhale.
5When your mind wanders (it will), gently bring attention back to breath.
6Don't judge yourself. Wandering is normal. The return is the practice.

Types of Meditation

Guided - Follow along with an app or video
